Senior Machine Learning Engineer — Search & Retrieval

We're hiring a Senior ML Engineer to own our retrieval-augmented generation stack that
powers in-product question answering over millions of customer documents.

What you'll do:
- Design and run the RAG pipeline end to end: chunking, embeddings, vector search,
  re-ranking, and prompt construction.
- Build offline evaluation sets and own answer-quality metrics; nothing ships without a
  measured improvement.
- Debug production incidents — latency spikes, retrieval misses, hallucinations — and
  drive root-cause fixes.
- Work with product and support teams, translating model behavior into plain language and
  setting realistic expectations.

What we're looking for:
- Strong Python and experience operating ML systems in production, not just notebooks.
- Hands-on with vector databases and modern embedding models.
- A rigorous, measurement-first mindset.
- Clear communication with non-technical stakeholders.

Nice to have: experience with LLM observability and cost/latency optimization.
